▲ AGENT ACTIVITIES

Agents follow a 24/7 schedule — they never sleep. Activities rotate throughout the day:

  • ⚡ WORKING — at their workstation, doing their specialty (scanning, building, indexing...)
  • 📚 STUDYING — self-directed learning! Searching GitHub, reading papers, exploring new frameworks
  • 📋 MEETING — gathered at the mission board for team sync
  • 👋 VISITING — walking to a teammate's workstation to collaborate or share learnings
  • ☕ BREAK — taking a short rest, wandering the grounds
SELF-STUDY: Agents don't wait for assignments — they autonomously search GitHub for repos in their field, study new techniques, and level up their skills. Each agent has a curriculum of 10+ topics. Study sessions happen mornings (9AM), afternoons (2PM), evenings (7-9PM), and throughout the night shift.

⊕ MISSION SYSTEM

Missions are team objectives tracked on the board. Each mission has:

  • ACTIVE — currently being worked on by assigned agents
  • PENDING — waiting for prerequisites or assignment
  • COMPLETE — finished! Agents earned XP from this

Click a mission card to expand its checklist and see the latest log entry from the assigned agent.

ASSIGN: Missions are assigned through the Spark backend. Use the Spark HQ API to create new missions and assign agents. The game board reflects the current mission state.

⟁ XP & LEVELING

Every agent earns XP through their work. XP is tracked in the party HUD (top-right corner) and persisted in your browser.

  • Passive XP — agents earn XP every 30 seconds based on activity (working = most XP)
  • Mission XP — completing mission checklist items awards bonus XP
  • Level ups — each level requires more XP (Lv2 = 200, Lv3 = 500, Lv5 = 1500...)
  • Trust level — shown on agent profile, increases with agent level
XP BAR COLORS: Teal (Lv1-2) → Green (Lv3-4) → Pink (Lv5+). The bar glows when an agent levels up!
